Purpose of the Project

Welcome to Alee's Ear Training! I made this quiz help me recognize different chord types that come from various sounds. Some sounds can make it hard to hear the chord as they have different qualities.

Select a quiz from the dropdown menu and press "Play Chord" to hear a chord. Try to identify the correct chord from the given options!

Use the progress bar to track your improvement as you go!
